Panomapper - Data Science

Innovative Data Science project focused on detecting solar panels from high-resolution satellite images. Harnessing the advancements of deep learning, we combined a CNN model, optimized for image classification and filtering out areas without solar panels, with a U-NET model, specially designed for precise segmentation of identified solar panels. Through meticulous topological mapping and advanced preprocessing of satellite images, we achieved outstanding accuracy in our detections. Among the standout features of the program, an interactive heatmap provides an intuitive visualization of solar panel distribution, and an analysis module estimates their total surface area in square meters.

Project Member : Xavier Soors, Samuel Etienne, Luc Rolland
